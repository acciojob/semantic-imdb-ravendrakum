Summary

The Shawshank Redemption is a 1994 American drama film based on the Stephen King novella "Rita Hayworth and Shawshank Redemption." It tells the story of Andy Dufresne, a banker who is sentenced to life in Shawshank State Penitentiary for a crime he didn't commit. Over the years, Andy forms a unique bond with fellow inmate Red and works to prove his innocence.

Actors

The film stars Tim Robbins as Andy Dufresne and Morgan Freeman as Ellis "Red" Redding.
